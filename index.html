Key topics shaping the 2025 Agenda

From deploying AI to forecast in chaos to navigating tariffs, rethinking assortments, and redefining loyalty in a world of algorithmic discovery, we’re digging into the mechanics of leading through complexity.

Data-Driven & AI-Augmented Retail Intelligence

How retailers and consumer brands are turning to adaptive AI and real-time data to forecast, plan, and operate with precision, navigating disruption, inflation, and supply chain volatility.

Product Curation & Innovation Under Pressure

How product, format, and merchandising teams are rethinking playbooks, flexing inventory, and making bold calls as they navigate volatility, supply chain strain, and platform complexity.

Brand Identity, Loyalty & Customer Experience in a Shifting World

How marketers, CX leaders, and loyalty strategists are building emotional connection and commercial impact amid cultural shifts, budget pressure, and the race to personalize at scale.

Leading Through Volatility & Change

How today’s executives are stabilizing while innovating, cutting costs while growing, and unifying while decentralizing, redefining leadership through the lens of geopolitics, labor, and organizational design.

Who Qualifies?

Senior executives who are employed by a specific retailer or consumer brand only, considered as an organization that manufactures and/or sells products direct to consumers either via direct channels or third parties. Executives that qualify must be:

  • Director level or above
  • Have decision making capabilities to source or influence technology solutions for their business
  • Possess and register with a corporate email address

All applications are subject to review and meeting the above criteria does not guarantee entry. Criteria may differ between Shoptalk events.
